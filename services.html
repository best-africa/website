Agribusiness and Agric-Market Development Services

This is enhancing the capacity Micro Small and Medium Enterprises along the agricultural value chain to effectively plan and manage business growth for sustainability. This is by making the MSMEs become attractive to their customers and bankable by developing their Agribusiness Management skills in the areas of:
• Entrepreneurship Development,
• Marketing and Business Strategy ... development,
• Small Farm Holder Financial Management,
• Strategic Business Planning.
The Agric-Market development Services involves conducting market research and enhancing the capacity of key market stakeholders through:
• Mobilizing and build the capacity of small agricultural producers to plan production and bulk harvest;
• Building farmer capacity to collectively clean, dry, store and process and seek buyers.
• Undertake Producer Surveys, Commodity Mapping leading to farmer-buyer linkages at local, regional and international levels.
